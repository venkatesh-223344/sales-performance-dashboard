# sales-performance-dashboard
Dashboard Overview

This is a Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard built on the SuperStore dataset.

KPIs
Total Sales
Total Profit
Profit Margin
Visualizations
Sales Trend Analysis (Line Chart)
Tracks sales over time (Year → Quarter → Month → Day).
Helps identify growth patterns and seasonality.
Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
Compares sales across categories such as Furniture, Technology, and Office Supplies.
Identifies top-performing product categories.
Profit by Region (Bar Chart)
Shows profitability across regions.
Helps management focus on high-profit markets.
Top Products by Sales (Bar Chart)
Displays best-selling products.
Useful for inventory and marketing decisions.
Filters
Region Slicer
Category Slicer

These allow users to interactively analyze specific segments.
